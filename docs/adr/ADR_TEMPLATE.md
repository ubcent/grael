## Context

- what is changing or being decided

## Decision

State the decision clearly and directly.

Prefer:

- "We will..."
- "Grael v1 will..."
- "The engine must..."

Avoid weak phrasing like:

- "We might..."
- "One option is..."
- "We are considering..."

---

## Alternatives Considered

List the serious alternatives and why they were not chosen.

For each alternative, explain:

- what it would have given us
- why it was rejected

## Consequences
